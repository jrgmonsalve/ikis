# BR-005: Credit Card Balance Handling

## Title

Credit card balance handling

## Business Rule

Credit card accounts must be handled as owed money, not as available money.

## Details

* A credit card account can be used to register expenses.
* Expenses made with a credit card increase the owed balance.
* Payments made to the credit card reduce the owed balance.
* Credit card owed balance must not be included in total available money.
* Credit card activity can be shown separately in reports and dashboards.

## Reason

A credit card does not represent available money. It represents debt generated by purchases.
