# BR-008: Transfer Is Not Income or Expense

## Title

Transfer is not income or expense

## Business Rule

A transfer between accounts must not be treated as income or expense.

## Details

* A transfer reduces the source account balance.
* A transfer increases the destination account balance.
* A transfer must not increase total family income.
* A transfer must not increase total family expenses.
* Transfers must be excluded from expense reports.
* Transfers must be excluded from income reports.
* Transfers must not affect budget usage.

## Reason

A transfer only moves money between accounts. It does not represent new money received or money spent.
