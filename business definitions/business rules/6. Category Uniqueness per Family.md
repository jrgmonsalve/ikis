# BR-006: Category Uniqueness per Family

## Title

Category uniqueness per family

## Business Rule

A family cannot have two active categories with the same name.

## Details

* Category names must be unique inside the same family.
* Two different families can have categories with the same name.
* Category comparison should ignore uppercase and lowercase differences.
* Inactive categories should not block the creation of a new category with the same name, unless the system supports reactivation.

## Reason

Duplicate categories create confusion in reports, budgets, and transaction classification.
