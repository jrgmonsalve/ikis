# BR-004: Account Balance Calculation

## Title

Account balance calculation

## Business Rule

An account balance must be calculated from the initial balance and the transactions associated with that account.

## Details

* The initial balance is used as the starting point.
* Income transactions increase the account balance.
* Expense transactions decrease the account balance.
* Transfers decrease the source account balance.
* Transfers increase the destination account balance.
* The current balance must always reflect the latest valid transactions.
* Reports and dashboards must use the calculated balance.

## Reason

The system must keep balances consistent and auditable.
