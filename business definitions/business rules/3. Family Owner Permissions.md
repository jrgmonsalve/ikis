# BR-003: Family Owner Permissions

## Title

Family owner permissions

## Business Rule

The family owner has full control over the family workspace.

## Details

* The family owner can invite new members.
* The family owner can remove members from the family.
* The family owner can manage family settings.
* The family owner can create, update, and deactivate accounts.
* The family owner can create, update, and deactivate categories.
* The family owner can create, update, and deactivate budgets.
* The family owner can create, update, and deactivate recurring payments.
* The family owner can view all family transactions and reports.
* The family owner cannot be removed by another member.

## Reason

Each family must have one user responsible for administration and ownership.
