# US-003: Invite a Family Member

## Title

Invite a family member

## User Story

As a family owner, I want to invite another user to my family, so that we can manage shared finances together.

## Acceptance Criteria

* The family owner can invite another person by email.
* The invited person receives an invitation to join the family.
* The invited person must authenticate before joining the family.
* Once accepted, the invited person becomes a family member.
* The new family member can access the family according to their assigned role.
* A user cannot join a family without accepting an invitation.

## Notes

This story only covers the invitation and joining process. Permissions will be refined in a separate story if needed.
