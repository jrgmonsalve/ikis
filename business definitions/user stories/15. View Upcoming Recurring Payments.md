# US-015: View Upcoming Recurring Payments

## Title

View upcoming recurring payments

## User Story

As a family member, I want to view upcoming recurring payments, so that the family can know which financial obligations are pending.

## Acceptance Criteria

* An authenticated family member can view upcoming recurring payments inside a family.
* The list shows active recurring payments.
* Each recurring payment shows its name.
* Each recurring payment shows its expected amount.
* Each recurring payment shows its next due date.
* Each recurring payment may show its suggested account.
* Each recurring payment may show its suggested category.
* Payments should be ordered by next due date.
* Paid recurring payments should not appear as pending for the current period.

## Notes

Example:

```
Internet
Expected Amount: $120,000
Next Due Date: 2026-06-15
Suggested Account: Bancolombia Savings
Suggested Category: Utilities
```
