# US-007: Register an Income

## Title

Register an income

## User Story

As a family member, I want to register an income, so that the account balance is updated and the family can track money received.

## Acceptance Criteria

* An authenticated family member can register an income inside a family.
* The income must have an amount.
* The income must be associated with an account.
* The income must be associated with a category.
* If the selected category has active subcategories, the income must be associated with one active subcategory.
* If the selected category has no active subcategories, the income is registered directly under the category.
* The income may have a description.
* The income may have a transaction date.
* If no transaction date is provided, the system uses the current date.
* When the income is saved, the selected account balance is increased.
* The income is included in reports and balance calculations.

## Notes

Example:

```
Amount: $3,500,000
Account: Bancolombia Savings
Category: Salary
Subcategory: Monthly salary
Description: Monthly salary
Date: 2026-06-03
```
