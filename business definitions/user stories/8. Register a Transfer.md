# US-008: Register a Transfer

## Title

Register a transfer between accounts

## User Story

As a family member, I want to register a transfer between accounts, so that I can move money from one account to another without treating it as income or expense.

## Acceptance Criteria

* An authenticated family member can register a transfer inside a family.
* The transfer must have an amount.
* The transfer must have a source account.
* The transfer must have a destination account.
* The source account and destination account must be different.
* The transfer may have a description.
* The transfer may have a transaction date.
* If no transaction date is provided, the system uses the current date.
* An active manual transfer can be edited by an active family member.
* Editing the transfer keeps the same transaction and adjusts account balances as needed.
* An active transfer can be cancelled by an active family member.
* Cancelling the transfer keeps the transaction in history and rolls back the transfer balance impacts.
* When the transfer is saved, the source account balance is reduced.
* When the transfer is saved, the destination account balance is increased.
* The transfer must not affect income or expense reports.

## Notes

Example:

```
Amount: $300,000
Source Account: Bancolombia Savings
Destination Account: Nequi
Description: Transfer to wallet
Date: 2026-06-03
```
