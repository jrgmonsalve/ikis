# UF-003: Invite a Family Member

## Related User Story

US-003: Invite a Family Member

## Goal

Allow a family owner to invite another person to join the family workspace.

## Main Flow

1. The family owner opens the family members section.
2. The system shows the current family members.
3. The family owner selects "Invite member".
4. The system shows the invitation form.
5. The family owner enters the invitee email.
6. The family owner selects the role for the invited member.
7. The family owner submits the invitation.
8. The system validates the email and role.
9. The system creates a pending invitation.
10. The system sends the invitation to the invitee.
11. The invitee opens the invitation.
12. The invitee signs in with Google.
13. The system validates the invitation.
14. The invitee accepts the invitation.
15. The system adds the invitee as a family member.
16. The system redirects the new member to the family dashboard.

## Alternative Flows

### Alternative Flow 1: Invalid email

1. The family owner enters an invalid email.
2. The system does not create the invitation.
3. The system shows a validation message.
4. The family owner corrects the email and submits again.

### Alternative Flow 2: Invitation already exists

1. The family owner enters an email that already has a pending invitation for the same family.
2. The system does not create a duplicate invitation.
3. The system shows a message indicating that the invitation is already pending.

### Alternative Flow 3: User is already a family member

1. The family owner enters the email of an existing family member.
2. The system does not create the invitation.
3. The system shows a message indicating that the user already belongs to the family.

### Alternative Flow 4: Invitation expired

1. The invitee opens an expired invitation.
2. The system does not allow the invitation to be accepted.
3. The system shows an expiration message.
4. The family owner can send a new invitation.

## Postconditions

* A pending invitation is created.
* Once accepted, the invited user becomes a family member.
* The new member can access the family according to the assigned role.
