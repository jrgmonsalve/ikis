# UF-008: Register a Transfer

## Related User Story

US-008: Register a Transfer

## Goal

Allow a family member to move money between accounts without treating the movement as income or
expense.

## Main Flow

1. The family member selects "Add transfer".
2. The system shows the transfer form.
3. The family member enters the transfer amount.
4. The family member selects the source account.
5. The family member selects the destination account.
6. The family member optionally enters a description.
7. The family member optionally selects a transaction date.
8. If no date is selected, the system uses the current date.
9. The family member submits the transfer.
10. The system validates the transfer information.
11. The system verifies that the source and destination accounts are different.
12. The system creates the transfer transaction.
13. The system reduces the source account balance.
14. The system increases the destination account balance.
15. The system confirms that the transfer was registered.

## Alternative Flows

### Alternative Flow 1: Same source and destination account

1. The family member selects the same account as source and destination.
2. The system does not create the transfer.
3. The system shows a validation message.
4. The family member selects a different destination account.

### Alternative Flow 2: Missing amount

1. The family member submits the form without an amount.
2. The system does not create the transfer.
3. The system shows a validation message.
4. The family member enters a valid amount.

### Alternative Flow 3: Missing source or destination account

1. The family member submits the form without selecting both accounts.
2. The system does not create the transfer.
3. The system shows a validation message.
4. The family member selects both accounts.

## Postconditions

* A transfer transaction is created.
* The source account balance is reduced.
* The destination account balance is increased.
* The transfer does not affect income, expense, or budget reports.
